Sally Grace Contarino

26 years old  ·  Melbourne, Victoria, Australia  ·  Brown hair  ·  Brown eyes  ·  Slim build

What We Know

  • Sally was last heard from by her family on June 20, 2026 — one day before her scheduled flight home to Melbourne.
  • She was travelling alone. She was a frequent and experienced solo traveller to Las Vegas and Palm Springs.
  • CCTV captured her at Timbers Bar & Grill, Las Vegas on the evening of June 19. She arrived on foot at 8:45 PM, had drinks, played gaming machines, and left alone at 10:28 PM walking eastbound along Cheyenne Street.
  • Staff reported she did not appear distressed — she seemed calm and was enjoying herself.
  • That same night she messaged friends: "I'm about to hike up Lone Mountain then go home — I fly back tomorrow."
  • She checked out of The Bungalows Hostel remotely via app — but never collected her luggage.
  • She had a Greyhound bus ticket booked from Las Vegas to Los Angeles for her return journey to Melbourne.
  • She never boarded her flight. She has not been seen or heard from since.
  • Her last known photo was taken at Mount Charleston, approximately 60km from Las Vegas.
