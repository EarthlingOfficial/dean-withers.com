⚠ LVMPD: Endangered Missing Adult — May Be Experiencing Severe Emotional Distress
Las Vegas, Nevada  ·  Southern California

Sally Grace Contarino

26 years old  ·  Melbourne, Victoria, Australia  ·  Brown hair  ·  Brown eyes  ·  Slim build

What We Know

  • June 17: Newly surfaced footage appears to show Sally at the 7-Eleven, 4880 Boulder Highway, Las Vegas at 3:46 PM.
  • June 19: Sally texted friends: "I'm about to hike up Lone Mountain then go home — I fly back tomorrow." CCTV later captured her at Timbers Bar & Grill from 8:45 PM to 10:28 PM. Detectives said she appeared calm before leaving alone on foot.
  • June 20: CCTV captured Sally returning to The Bungalows Hostel. She told staff she would return for her luggage. She then visited a café she regularly went to in Las Vegas, ordered breakfast and a protein smoothie, then boarded a bus to Mount Charleston.
  • Police told the family Sally got off the bus and walked into the park. Investigators believe she began a six-hour hike from the Mount Charleston Visitor Center — where she never checked in.
  • Her Greyhound bus ticket to Los Angeles was never scanned. No CCTV shows her boarding the bus.
  • Police have recovered Sally's luggage and conducted searches using dogs with her clothing and a grid search of the area.
  • Note: The Mount Charleston photo released by LVMPD was taken during a previous Las Vegas trip, not her current one. It was included in the official missing person bulletin.
  • LVMPD have released CCTV footage of Sally walking with a bag slung across her shoulder and issued an urgent appeal describing her as an "endangered missing adult" who may be "experiencing severe emotional distress" and in need of medical attention.
  • Last seen wearing: Grey or blue sweatshirt, denim shorts, black and white shoes, carrying a bag.

New Footage — Real Vegas Locals

A newly surfaced video may show one of the last public recordings of Sally before she disappeared. The original video was shared with Real Vegas Locals by @epicdamianpov. Its metadata shows it was recorded on June 17, 2026 at 3:46 PM, with GPS coordinates matching the 7-Eleven at 4880 Boulder Highway, Las Vegas.
