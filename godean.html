System Log // About

The Game That
Reality Builds

GO DEAN! is an ethical vegan horror project based on the Backrooms film 2026 — those infinite, liminal corridors where the wallpaper never ends and the fluorescent lights never stop flickering. But this backrooms has a twist: it evolves in real time, fed by actual debates happening in the world above.

The Backrooms Conceit

You're trapped in a maze of faded yellow walls and humming lights. The maze is Dean Withers' logic. Every corridor is an argument. Every dead end is a dodge. You — the Earthling — must find the exit.

Vegan Horror

This isn't jump scares. It's the slow, creeping horror of cognitive dissonance — the discomfort that comes when someone is confronted with the reality of what they fund every single day.

Real-World Engine

Every new debate between the Earthling and Dean Withers generates a new level. The game grows. The maze deepens. The walls close in. The discourse is the content.

Ethical Foundation

Built on the principle that animals are not ours to use. The horror isn't fictional — it happens billions of times a day. GO DEAN! makes you feel that truth.

Trapped Inside

Dean Withers

Dean Withers has been entered into the game. He's in there now — wandering the backrooms, surrounded by his own arguments. Every time we debate in the real world, the game listens. A new level spawns. New corridors. New logic traps. New horror.

  1. The Earthling challenges Dean Withers to a debate on veganism in the real world.
  2. The debate happens — live, recorded, in comments, wherever truth meets resistance.
  3. Key arguments, deflections and fallacies are extracted from the debate.
  4. A new backrooms level is architected from Dean's logic — each room a claim, each corridor a dodge.
  5. The level goes live. Players navigate Dean's maze. They find the exit: the truth.
  6. More debates. More levels. The backrooms grows. Dean runs further. We follow.
